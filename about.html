Brishid / About

A premium ingredient brand with a trader’s mindset.

Brishid was built to connect sourcing discipline with café creativity. The mission is to bring better beverage ingredients to businesses that want better drinks and stronger margins.

Brand story

Brishid is not trying to be another powder seller.

The brand is built around a simple belief: cafés do not only need ingredients; they need ingredients that help them create menu value. That is why Brishid combines product sourcing, drink testing, visual branding, and B2B support.

Brand principles

1
Quality must be useful

A premium ingredient must perform in real café drinks.

2
Education creates trust

Clear recipes and explanations make the buyer more confident.

3
Branding increases value

The way a product is presented changes how much customers want it.

Vision

To become a trusted premium beverage ingredient brand for cafés and specialty drink businesses — starting with matcha and cacao, expanding with discipline.
