About Barista Matcha

Our AAA Barista Matcha has a vivid green hue and a deep, robust flavor that stands up well to milk and other ingredients. It offers a balanced combination of savory umami and fresh, grassy undertones, making it ideal for lattes, smoothies, and baking. This matcha is naturally cultivated and stone-ground to ensure high quality.

Serving Suggestions

Blend one teaspoon of barista matcha with a splash of hot water to form a smooth paste. Add your choice of milk and sweetener, then whisk or shake until frothy. Enjoy your matcha latte hot or over ice. You can also add barista matcha to smoothies, shakes, or baked goods to infuse them with a nutrient-rich, vibrant green boost.
