Ceremonial Grade Matcha

Experience the finest ceremonial matcha, vibrant in color and rich in tradition.

About Ceremonial Matcha

Ceremonial matcha is the highest grade of matcha, made from the youngest tea leaves that are shade‑grown to enhance their sweetness. These leaves are carefully stone-ground into a fine, bright green powder with a subtle sweetness and umami flavor. Ceremonial matcha has a delicate flavor profile with little bitterness, making it perfect for traditional tea ceremonies and for enjoying on its own.

Our Ceremonial Grade Matcha is sourced from select farms and carefully processed to ensure maximum freshness and quality. Each tin delivers a smooth, vibrant brew that reflects centuries of Japanese tea heritage.

How to Prepare

Use a bamboo whisk to gently combine ceremonial matcha with hot water (approximately 80°C). Whisk in an "M" motion until a frothy layer forms on top. Enjoy immediately to appreciate the fresh aroma and rich, creamy flavor.
